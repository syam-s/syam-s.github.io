I received my masters (M. Tech) in turbomachines from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat in 2013 and bachelors from Aeronautical Society of India, Newdelhi in 2011 in Aeronautical Engineering.

Areas of expertise include CFD, experimental studies, and Fortran.
